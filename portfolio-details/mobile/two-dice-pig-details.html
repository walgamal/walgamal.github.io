Two-Dice Pig

Project information

  • Category: Mobile App
  • Completion Date: March 11, 2022
  • Technologies Used: Java, Android Studio, Android Animation, XML
  • GitHub URL: Two-Dice Pig Repo

This android mobile application is a two-player dice game. Two-Dice Pig involves rolling two dice, accumulating points, and making strategic decisions to win. Each player takes turns rolling the dice, and the points from each roll are added to a turn total. If a player rolls a one on either die, their turn total resets to zero, and it becomes the next player's turn. However, if a player rolls two ones (snake eyes), their overall score resets to zero. Players can choose to hold their current turn total, adding it to their overall score. The first player to reach or exceed 50 points wins the game. Additionally, the game includes animations for dice rolling and visual indicators for the current player and winner.
